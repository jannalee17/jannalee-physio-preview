How it works

Three steps, no guesswork

You don't have to have it all figured out before you walk in. That's our job — here's how we'll work through it together.

1

Book & assessment

You book online or by phone — no referral needed. At your first visit we listen to your story, watch how you move, and start hunting for what's really driving the problem.

2

Your personalized plan

We build a plan around your goals and your life — not a one-size-fits-all handout. Hands-on treatment, movement, and a few things to do at home that actually fit your week.

3

Recovery & performance

As pain settles, we shift toward strength and confidence so you can get back to what you love — and stay there. You don't have to stop; sometimes we just have to modify.
