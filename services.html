Other ways we help you move

Custom Orthotics

Sometimes the thing driving your knee, hip, or back pain starts at your feet. We cast and fit custom orthotics to support how you actually stand, walk, and move — built from a mould of your own foot, not off a shelf.

If you're not sure whether orthotics would help you, that's a great thing to bring up at an assessment. We'll take a look at how you're moving first, then talk through whether they make sense for you.

Group Exercise Classes

Moving feels better with company. Our kinesiology-led group classes keep you strong, steady, and connected — without the pressure of a big-box gym.

Age Defying Fitness is built for adults 55+ who want to hold onto their strength and balance and keep doing their own thing. After Hours Fitness runs in the evenings for busy adults who can't make it in during the day. Age is just a number — we'll meet you where you are and modify as we go.
