Meet Our Team in Yorkton, Saskatchewan

Registered physiotherapists and kinesiologists who'll remember your name and treat the whole you — not just the body part that hurts. Many of the people we see end up sending their kids, partners, and parents to us too.

Physiotherapy

Our Physiotherapists

Jannalee Edgar

Physiotherapist · Owner
BMR PT · Certificate in Sports Physiotherapy

Jannalee is the owner of Jannalee Physiotherapy. She grew up in a small farming community in rural Manitoba and graduated from the University of Manitoba in 2013 with a Bachelor of Medical Rehabilitation – Physiotherapy, earning the Dr. Ron Bowie Award for her contribution to the community. When she moved to Yorkton, she knew it was important to carry that same philosophy with her. She volunteers her first aid and physiotherapy services to Yorkton Minor Football, and can be found at Westland Arena working with the U18 AAA and U18 AA hockey teams — she's also covered provincial gymnastics, rugby, and CrossFit competitions. Most recently she completed her Certificate in Sports Physiotherapy. Jannalee grew up in athletics, competing from grassroots to the national level, and still loves to stay active — these days with running and CrossFit.

Darshil Kheni

Physiotherapist
Bachelor of Physiotherapy

Darshil Kheni is an internationally trained physiotherapist with a Bachelor of Physiotherapy from Veer Narmad South Gujarat University, India (2016–2021), where he was honoured with the Excellent Student Award in 2022 for his dedication to teaching and advancing the profession. After graduating, he owned and worked as the consultant physiotherapist at an outpatient clinic in India. Now based in Canada, Darshil is focused on continuing to grow and sharpen his skills. In his free time, he enjoys making delicious sweets and playing carrom.

Nidhi Patel

Physiotherapist
Bachelor of Physiotherapy

Nidhi Patel is an internationally trained physiotherapist with a Bachelor of Physiotherapy from Ahmedabad Institute of Medical Sciences, Gujarat University, India (2016–2021). She gained valuable experience as a senior physical therapist in outpatient care, working with strength and conditioning, manual therapy, and myofascial release. Nidhi has completed certifications including Basic Life Support (AHA guidelines), a Paediatric Physiotherapy certification, and the Physiotherapy Practice in the Canadian Health Care System certification. She believes in working alongside her clients to help them move well and get back to a healthy, active lifestyle.
